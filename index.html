Food Glossary

Biryani
- A rice dish cooked with spices and meat or vegetables.
Tandoori
- A dish cooked in a traditional soil called Tandoor.
Lassi
- A refreshing yogurt-based drink, sweet or salty.
Chutney
- A tangy semiliquid made with herbs, fruits, or vegetables.
Korma
- A Mughlai curry made with yogurt, cream, nuts, and spices.
Falooda
- A cold dessert with milk, rose syrup, vermicelli, and ice cream.
